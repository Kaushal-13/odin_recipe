Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is a food typically consisting of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between slices of bread, or more generally any dish wherein bread serves as a container or wrapper for another food type.

The sandwich began as a portable, convenient finger food in the Western world, though over time it has become prevalent worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables/Fillings of your choice
  • Pan and oil if you intend to heat

Recipe

  2. Place the vegetables inside two slices of bread
  3. Toast if you like
